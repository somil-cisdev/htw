Wellness Consulting

Beauty and lifestyle are interconnected, with lifestyle choices significantly influencing a person's appearance and overall well-being. Beauty encompasses more than just aesthetics, encompassing physical, emotional, and spiritual health, which are integral to a balanced lifestyle. In essence, a healthy and well-rounded lifestyle can enhance beauty, while beauty practices and choices can contribute to a more fulfilling life.

Wellness Coaching Services

Lifestyle Wellness Coaching

Our coaches measure all outcomes and use guided intervention and behavioral strategies that are empathic, mindful, and motivational to improve self-care and lifestyle change. This includes weight loss strategies; increasing energy; heart disease prevention; reduction of musculoskeletal joint pain and stiffness; and decreasing whole-body inflammation associated with natural aging, arthritis, and diabetes.

Wellness Coaching Services

Anti-Aging Longevity Coaching 

This growing field focuses on helping individuals improve their overall health and potentially extend their healthspan through personalized longevity strategies.  These coaches often work with clients to adopt a "pro-aging" lifestyle, emphasizing healthy habits and preventative measures, rather than just focusing on reversing the side-effects of aging.

Corporate Wellness Coaching

The success of any business rides on the well-being of its employees. As productivity losses, absenteeism, and healthcare costs rise, the need to create a healthier workplace culture has never been more important. Scarce resources and lack of expertise may make engaging in a wellness program a daunting task- our team is here to support!

Wellness Coaching Services

Mind-Body Balance Coaching

The mind-body connection is a two-way street, where the mind affects the body and the body affects the mind. We teach self-care strategies and techniques that break down stuck energies from unprocessed emotions and traumas, transforming them within. When this occurs, the way you feel and think begins to change, and your energy flows the way it was designed.

Coaching Services

Beauty & Skincare Coaching

Skin care is a crucial aspect of improving natural beauty and overall health. A healthy skin care routine includes external factors like preventing sun damage and absorbing toxins in the air. It also includes internal factors like hydration and increased microcirculation to the skin, which increases the delivery of oxygen, nutrients, removal of waste products, and lymphatic drainage, all of which contribute to healthy and vibrant skin.

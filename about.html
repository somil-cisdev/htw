Meet Brittany

Brittany L. Wilson – Health & Wellness Coach / B.S. A.S.

Brittany L. Wilson, a native of Fresno, California, born in 1984, established a foundation in academics and athletics at an early age. Her educational journey, marked by a passion for math and science, led her to the University of California, Irvine, where she graduated in Biological Sciences and Cognitive Psychology in 2006. Brittany’s career path initially meandered through various roles, including tutoring, chemistry research, and emergency medical work, before settling in the real estate sector in 2007.

Brittany’s life took a dramatic turn following two serious car accidents, one in 2009 and again in 2016, which left her grappling with physical injuries and mental health challenges. These experiences, while daunting, catalyzed her journey towards holistic healing and personal transformation. Embracing both Western and Eastern medical practices, including sound and frequency healing, Brittany overcame her ailments and emerged as a health and wellness coach. Her personal recovery and professional growth led her to integrate innovative medical technologies into her practice and expand her expertise into life and spiritual coaching. Through these endeavors, Brittany is committed to guiding others in transforming their lives, drawing from her profound personal challenges and triumphs.

About Us

HealthTech Wellness: How it began

Cutting-edge technology, traditional Western Medicine, and ancient Eastern Medicine are integrating at an exceptionally fast rate. HealthTech Wellness was founded to guide you to the most profound, natural medical and health products and services.

We’ve created a safe space to educate, clarify, and correct some of the misinformation within the health and wellness industry. Your protocol is personalized so that you can start making the necessary changes to thrive in today’s economy. Find the balance that is as unique as you are! We know that’s possible, because t is out of complete desperation- after more than a decade of suffering from chronic pain, migraines, anxiety, depression, post-traumatic stress and insomnia- that the search for the latest and greatest medical advances were found.

HealthTech Wellness is here to lead the way to breakthrough health and wellness technology and coach you through your challenges; overcome past pitfalls and traumas; explore alternative treatments; and offer the most profound, technologically advanced products and services. This is all done while keeping the foundation focused on intention and purpose, creating conscious connections with others, and going ”back to the basics” with the 4 pillars of health: 1) diet; 2) physical activity; 3) restorative sleep; and 4) positive mindset.

What is AVACEN®?

AVACEN® is a drug-free, noninvasive, over-the-counter (OTC) heat therapy system indicated for improvement of local circulation in muscle tissue in as little as 20-minutes of treatment, facilitating, and improving microcirculation throughout the entire body. Since 2009, the benefits of AVACEN devices have impacted thousands of people worldwide, with over 30 million safe treatments to date. Advanced Vascular Circulation Enhancement (AVACEN) is safe, effective, and easy to use. It doesn't matter if you're 2 or 100 years of age, AVACEN is for everyone!

  • Joint Pain Associated With Arthritis
  • Muscle spasms

THE SCIENCE

SINGLE POINT TREATMENT

With patented, FDA Cleared Class II medical devices, AVACEN's cutting edge technology combines heat therapy with negative pressure to increase local circulation into a unique vascular network, located in the palm of the hand.

Raising the blood temperature at this "single point treatment" (the palm) increases blood temperature throughout the body naturally via the circulatory system.

This can benefit the entire body! Our breakthrough heat therapy technology offers a simple, affordable, easy-to-use solution to address your health concerns.
